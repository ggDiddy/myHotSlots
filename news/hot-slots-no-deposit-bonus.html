How Wagering Requirements Actually Work — And How to Beat Them

Most players leave bonus money on the table because they misread the terms. Here is a plain-English breakdown of the numbers that matter:

Wagering multiplier: The number of times you must bet the bonus amount (or bonus + deposit). A 1x requirement is the gold standard. Anything over 35x is a red flag.

Game contribution: Slots typically contribute 100% toward wagering requirements. Table games often contribute just 10–20%. Always play slots if you are trying to clear a no deposit bonus fast.

Time limit: Most no deposit bonuses expire in 7–30 days. Missing the deadline means forfeiting both the bonus and any accumulated winnings.

Maximum bet rule: Nearly all casinos cap your bet size while playing through a bonus — usually $5–$10 per spin. Exceeding this limit can void your winnings entirely. This is the rule most players accidentally break.
